About us

King's Noodles brings together the finest Vietnamese noodle dishes, from traditional pho and refreshing bun to flavorful hu tieu. We take pride in introducing you to the unique tastes of each region, offering an authentic culinary experience that captures the true spirit of Vietnam.

At King's Noodles, each bowl of pho, bun, or hu tieu is carefully crafted to preserve the traditional flavors of Vietnam. We aim to take you on a journey through our country’s rich culinary heritage, bringing you closer to the essence of each noodle dish across this beautiful land.
